Number of Songs in Each Musical Key Bar Chart

This visualization is interactive! Hover over a bar to see the number of songs in that key.

Description

The bar chart displays the distribution of songs across musical keys, categorized by mode (major or minor). The musical keys are represented along the x-axis, while the y-axis shows the number of songs in each key. The bars are stacked to show the breakdown between major and minor modes, with the blue portion representing major and the red portion representing minor. Major keys are generally associated with more upbeat, energetic, and positive music, which is reflected in the higher number of songs in keys like A and G♯/A♭. On the other hand, minor keys tend to convey a more melancholic, sad, or introspective tone, as seen in keys like A♯/B♭ and D# / E♭, where the minor mode dominates. Overall, while major keys are more prevalent in this dataset, the minor keys still make up a significant portion, highlighting the diversity of emotional expression in music across different keys.
